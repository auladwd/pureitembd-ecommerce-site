## Customization

To modify products:

1. Go to `/admin/products`
2. Click edit icon on any product
3. Update details (title, price, stock, images, etc.)
4. Save changes

To add new products:

1. Go to `/admin/products`
2. Click "Add Product" button
3. Fill in product details
4. Upload images (comma-separated URLs)
5. Save
